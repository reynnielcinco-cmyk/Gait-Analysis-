Precision Engineering: Inside the Gait Analysis System

A detailed look at the cutting-edge hardware and software that delivers clinical-grade accuracy.

Detailed Technical Breakdown

Sensor Technology

The system utilizes Inertial Measurements Units(IMUs) with integrated 9-axis motion tracking (accelerometer, gyroscope, and magnetometer) paired with high-resolution. Force Sensing Resistors (FSRs) for plantar pressure mapping.

Acquisition Rate

Data streams are synchronized and captured at a high-speed sampling rate of 200 Hz, ensuring no micro-movement or rapid transient force is missed, providing unmatched smoothness and detail.

Key Data Parameters

  • Spatial:Stride/Step Length, Step Width, Foot Angle
  • Temporal:Stance/Swing Time, Cadence, Double Support Time
  • Kinetics:Pressure Maps, Peak Force, Force-Time Integral
